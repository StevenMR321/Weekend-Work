Brasileirao and Premier League

Among the best leagues in the world according to the IFFHS.

TheInternational Federation of Football History and Statistics (IFFHS)
revealed the ranking of the best leagues in the world in 2022.
The Brasileirao, or Brazil's Serie A, was positioned
first in the table for the second time in the history of the
annual ranking IFFHS launched in 1991.

The English Premier League, which was first in 2019, is positioned
in the second box for the fifth time in six years. Spanish LaLiga,
which rises one place, is third ahead of the Bundesliga. The Italian
Serie A loses two places and is ahead of the French Ligue 1.
